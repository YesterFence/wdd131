Overview

Purpose

What makes this website unique is that what website have you heard of that is exclusively cheese?
Many people would love to just focus on Cheeses and not have other worldly things bombard them for their time of bliss.

Audience

The website will talk about how cheese is loved by many people, even those that are lactose intolerant.
Itâ€™s dedicated to educating, inspiring, and connecting cheese lovers to one website. This website is the one-stop destination for all things cheese, ranging from cheese varieties, pairing recommendations, cooking tips, and even prices of cheeses.

Dynamic elements

I will randomly display pictures and prices of cheese when loading a webpage. Implementing a button to order range of prices from low to high. I'll have a button to ask the user how much of each cheese they would like to purchase. I will use functions to calculate the total prices of cheese and display it. Maybe use the same webpage and hide the "recipt" behind the page and when asked to check-out, bring it to the foreground.
